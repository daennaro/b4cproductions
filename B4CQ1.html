CHARACTERISTICS OF A GOOD STREAM

A characteristic of a good stream is keeping your stream schedule consistent and having lots of information to provide. The video and audio quality should be good. This means having a clear and stable picture, and clear and audible audio. The streaming content should be engaging and keep viewers interested. This can be done by using a variety of techniques, such as storytelling, humor, and interaction with the audience.good and stable content, that constantly makes the viewers engaging and fun to watch or listen to. This also of course includes monthly or daily uploads to your stream, this also applies to streaming apps such as Netflix, Spotify, Youtube, etc. They all constantly upload content in their own ways.
