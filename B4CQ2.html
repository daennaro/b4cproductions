The stream will start at 5PM where our target audience (Teenagers/Adolescents) will most Likely be free to watch. The introduction will start with the members introducing the topic of our stream for 10 minutes.

The stream will then continue to breaking down the topic to the audience, starting from:

  1. How social media affects people's relationships.
  2. Possible social media sites that people tend to become addicted to.
  3. How Tiktok can become addictive.
  4. How Tiktok addiction can ruin a person's relationships.
  5. How Tiktok can ruin a teen's relationship with their parents.

After breaking down the topic to the audience by laying our and presenting the research done on the topic, we will proceed to entertain the viewers questions and comments.
